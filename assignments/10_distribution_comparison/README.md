# Assignment 10 — Distribution Comparison

## Problem
Compare four discrete probability distributions,
compute key statistics and confidence intervals.

## Distributions
| Distribution | EX | DX  | Std Dev | Interval         |
|--------------|----|-----|---------|------------------|
| Uniform      | 20 | 140 | 11.83   | [8.17, 31.83]    |
| Binomial     | 20 | 10  | 3.16    | [16.84, 23.16]   |
| Poisson      | 20 | 20  | 4.47    | [15.53, 24.47]   |
| Geometric    | 20 | 420 | 20.49   | [-0.49, 40.49]   |

## Key Observation
Same mean (EX=20), very different spread:
Geometric >> Uniform >> Poisson >> Binomial
